Yogurt Bowl

Sit down with a nice book, a hot cup of tea, and dig into this deliciously healthy breakfast bowl!

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup - Chobani Vanilla Greek Yogurt
  • 1 - Sliced Green Kiwi
  • 1/4 Cup - Frozen Blueberries
  • 1/4 Cup - Muesli of choice
  • 1 TBSP - Crunchy Peanut Butter
  • 1 TSP - Local Honey

Method

This may just be the easiest recipe out there.

There are no instructions! Just assemble the ingredients and you are ready to eat :)
